Our History

Founded in 1902, Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is one of the most successful and renowned football clubs in the world. Known for their iconic white kits, the club has established itself as a symbol of excellence and dominance in football.

Real Madrid has been crowned European champions a record 14 times, showcasing their legacy in the UEFA Champions League. The club has also won 35 La Liga titles, making it the most successful team in Spanish football history.

Throughout its history, Real Madrid has been home to some of the greatest football players, including Alfredo Di Stéfano, Ferenc Puskás, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Zinedine Zidane. The Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, their iconic home ground, stands as a testament to their rich heritage and passion for football.

Real Madrid's commitment to excellence extends beyond the field, with a strong fan base worldwide and a dedication to upholding the values of sportsmanship and unity.
